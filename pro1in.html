NSS Programs

Our programs focus on community service and development. Here are some of the key areas where NSS volunteers can make a significant impact:

  • Waste Management: Proper segregation of biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste.
  • Recycling Programs: Sorting recyclable materials like plastics, paper, and metals.
  • Organizing Community Events: Efficiently sorting and managing tasks for volunteers during events.
  • Food Distribution: Sorting and distributing food to the needy in an organized manner.
  • Library Management: Sorting books and maintaining an efficient library system.
  • Environmental Cleanup: Organizing cleanup drives and sorting collected waste.
